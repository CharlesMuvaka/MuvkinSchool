Welcome to MuvkinSchool

Where Programming lives.

Muvkin School was founded in 2022 in Kenya. It was began due to the increasing demands from technology without people to implement it. The original thought was to make it a free technological school used to support street children across the country. Other students willing to cater for all the costs are also welcomed inorder to support diversity in the school.

To promote the schools culture students must sign in to do the opening term quiz which consists of five questions.
